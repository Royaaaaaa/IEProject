  • Be a friend of the Marine animals, be a Victorian Wildlife Bunj!

    The word 'Bunj'is an Aboriginal word for 'Mate', usually used when addressing a friend. Sometimes used as 'Bunji/Bunjee'.

  • Do Marine animals need our help?

    Animals are found trapped in plastics in beach clean-ups. Five trillion pieces of plastics are floating in our sea.

  • Is plastic fantastic?

    Plastic is made from oil, a limited resource. When we design one-time use items with plastic, we are using a valuable material in an irresponsible way. Choose reusable items today. We all can do something to help.
